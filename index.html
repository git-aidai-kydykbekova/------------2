An unknown waterfall in the Cholpon-Ata gorge

A fabulous wonderland

The UNKNOWN CHOLPON-ATA WATERFALL is the most mysterious and beautiful waterfall on the northern shore of Lake Issyk-Kul! A walking path of 5 km in one direction, a gravel path, an ascent of 700 meters. The height of the waterfall is about 40 meters. The height above sea level is 2600 m. The water falls in an incredibly powerful stream, practically forming foam, this powerful energy will not leave anyone indifferent.

The tour participants will have a professional photo shoot from the best photographers! After all, people from all over the world come here for magical photos!

We will arrange an amazing day full of impressions:

  • —— We will take many, many positive gorgeous photos, walk along beautiful bridges and arrange a vacation at the most mysterious waterfall!
  • — We will see blooming mountain grasses and a panorama overlooking Lake Issyk-Kul

Hotel

The Chaika Resort Recreation Center is located on the northern shore of Lake Issyk-Kul, Sary-Oi village, 14 km from Cholpon-Ata, 10 km from Tamchy Issyk Kul Airport and 230 km from Bishkek. Comfortable rooms of the standard, superior standard and Junior Suites categories. As well as family rooms, townhouses and apartments are equipped with everything you need. Extensive territory with landscaping. Some rooms have balconies with lake and mountain views.

There is a restaurant, a bar, and a children's playground on the territory of the boarding house. Chaika Resort Boarding house is an ideal place for those who want to take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy the beauty of the nature of the Issyk–Kul coast. There is also a 3–course meal from the Social Coffee - Breeze restaurant chains, which will surprise you with its variety of dishes.

Fresh sea air, a beautiful view from the window and a cup of delicious coffee! You will find it right here at the Chaika Resort recreation center!
